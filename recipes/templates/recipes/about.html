About Sip n Savor

Welcome to Sip n Savor, your go-to resource for all things cocktails!

Functionality

  • View Trending Cocktails - See what cocktails are popular right now
  • View Popular Cocktails - Discover the most popular cocktails of all time
  • View New Cocktails - Find the latest cocktail recipes
  • Search for Cocktails by Name - Search for specific cocktail recipes by name
  • Advanced Search - Use ingredients, tags, mixer, and glass type to search for the perfect cocktail recipe
  • Create User Account - Create an account to save your favorite recipes and ingredients
  • Save Recipes - Save your favorite cocktail recipes for future reference
  • Save Ingredients to MyBar - Keep track of your favorite ingredients and easily find cocktail recipes that use them
  • Search for Cocktails based on MyBar Ingredients - Find cocktail recipes that you can make with the ingredients in your bar
